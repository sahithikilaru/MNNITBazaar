FAQs

What do I write in "Title"

For books mention the title of the book, subject and the author. For cycles mention the company and the model. For any other item the common name will do.

What to mention in "Description"

Mention the age of the item, its condition and any other relevant information. Be truthful and precise.

What to fill in "Category"

Fill in one of the following - "books", "cycles", "electronics", "household". For any other category fill "other".

What are some good image logo practices?

Have a resolution of 512x512. Use common image formats such as .JPG, .GIF, or .PNG. Remain under the 2MB limit. Square images look best.

What is the "Ask" field

It is used for filling the asking price. This is the base price at which the auction begins.

What is the format of "Closes"

YYYY-MM-DD HH-MM-SS. Where Y=year, M=month, D=day, H=hours, M=minutes, S=seconds. It denotes the closing time of the auction.
